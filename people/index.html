Lawrence & Long Architects

Joe Lawrence

Joe Lawrence has been a member of the RIAI since 1999 and is a current member of the AAI (Architectural Association of Ireland) and an RIAI accredited, Conservation Grade II Architect.

Prior to setting up Lawrence and Long Architects, Joe worked with architectural offices in Australia and the United States. In Dublin, Joe worked at de Blacam and Meagher Architects on a variety of award winning residential, retail and office projects, which have won RIAI, AAI and Europa Nostra Awards.

Pierre Long

Pierre Long is a member of RIAI and RIBA (Royal Institute of British Architects) and teaches at the School of Architecture at University College Dublin. Pierre has worked in Paris , London and on award winning projects with De Blacam and Meagher Archtiects throughout Ireland prior to setting up Lawrence and Long Architects.

Pierre has a particular interest in educational and residential projects through his involvement with projects at Roscrea College, University College Dublin and Galway Mayo Institute of Technology.

Amélie Conway
